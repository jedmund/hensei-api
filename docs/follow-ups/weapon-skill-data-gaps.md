# Weapon skill data gaps

- Mysterious ATK and Mysterious VIT are limited to collaboration-event weapons we do not own. Their real panel values cannot currently be captured, so leave them unverified until another primary source is available.
- The complete eight-weapon Rose Crystal series is curated from its two shared profiles. The Bow and Knife SL10 captures validate one member of each profile; the series documentation supplies the shared Brier/Bud and Thorns/Barrier rules. Each protected element has its own reduction bucket so mixed Rose grids do not merge unrelated reductions.
